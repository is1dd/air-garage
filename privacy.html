Further information about the processing of Personal Data

Fullstory

Fullstory is a web analysis and session tracking service provided by FullStory, Inc. (“Fullstory”). Fullstory utilizes the Data collected to track and examine the use of AirGarage and to prepare reports on its activities. FullStory’s purpose in collecting Usage Information is to better understand how Users use AirGarage. Personal Data collected: pages visited, links clicked, non-sensitive text entered, mouse movements, referring URL, browser, operating system, cookie information, and Internet Service Provider. Place of processing: US – Privacy Policy – Opt Out.

Pseudonymous use

When registering for AirGarage, Users have the option to indicate a nickname or pseudonym. In this case, Users' Personal Data shall not be published or made publicly available. Any activity performed by Users on AirGarage shall appear in connection with the indicated nickname or pseudonym. However, Users acknowledge and accept that their activity on AirGarage, including content, information or any other material possibly uploaded or shared on a voluntary and intentional basis may directly or indirectly reveal their identity.

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13

Users declare themselves to be adult according to their applicable legislation. Minors may use AirGarage only with the assistance of a parent or guardian. Under no circumstance persons under the age of 13 may use AirGarage.

Unique device identification

AirGarage may track Users by storing a unique identifier of their device, for analytics purposes or for storing Users' preferences.

User identification via a universally unique identifier (UUID)

AirGarage may track Users by storing a so-called universally unique identifier (or short UUID) for analytics purposes or for storing Users' preferences. This identifier is generated upon installation of this Application, it persists between Application launches and updates, but it is lost when the User deletes the Application. A reinstall generates a new UUID.
